##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Marinara Sauce:

1. Prepare 1 can tomatoes
1. Take 1 pack tomato sauce (250ml)
1. Prepare 1 tsp Italian spice
1. Prepare 5 tbsp olive oil
1. Make ready 1 tsp white sugar
1. Make ready 1 tsp salt
1. Get 1 tbsp finely ground pepper
1. Make ready 1 cloves garlic finely chopped
1. Get 1 onion finely chopped
1. Get 2 tbsp parmigian cheese (optional)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Easy Marinara Sauce:

1. In a large skillet pour in the olive oil then add garlic, onions, can tomatoes, tomato sauce and the  Italian spices. Stir occasionally for 10 to 15 minutes. optionally, you can use blender to mix ingredients for a smoother sauce.
1. Add the parmigian cheese, then salt and pepper to taste. Simmer for 5 to 10 minutes. Can be use for pizza, calzone and other pasta dishes.
