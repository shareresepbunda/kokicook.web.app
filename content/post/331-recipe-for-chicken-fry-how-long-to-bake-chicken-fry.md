##### The ingredients needed to make Chicken Fry:

1. Take 28 Pieces Chicken Legs
1. Get 20 tbs Red chilli powder
1. Prepare 4 tbs Pepper powder
1. Take 2 tsp Turmeric Powder
1. Make ready 6 tbs Garam Masala
1. Take 8 tbs Tandoori Chicken Masala
1. Take 1/2 Lemon&#39;s Juice
1. Get  Water as required for marination
1. Prepare  Salt as required
1. Prepare  Oil to shallow-medium fry
1. Take  Curry leaves (Few)
1. Prepare  Coriander leaves to garnish
1. Take 1 L/XL Ziploc bag

##### Instructions to make Chicken Fry:

1. In a deep bowl add all the masalas and mix the lemon and a few drops of water as required to marinate the chicken pieces.
1. Add the pieces one by one and coat each pieces.
1. The coated pieces should be kept and stored in a Ziploc bag for 24hrs in the refrigerator.
1. Take a wok/deep fry pan pour oil (I added oil for a medium fry) and bring it to a medium heat.
1. Add the curry leaves and start adding the pieces one by one as much as 5-6 pieces in the first round and fry them. Repeat it as required
1. Serve them in a platter or tray and add Chopped Coriander for garnish
