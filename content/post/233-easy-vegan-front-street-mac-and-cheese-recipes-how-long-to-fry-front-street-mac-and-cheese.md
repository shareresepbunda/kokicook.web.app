##### The ingredients needed to make Front Street Mac and Cheese:

1. Prepare 1/3 cup onion, finely diced
1. Take 1 tbsp. garlic, minced
1. Prepare 3 tbsp. butter
1. Prepare 3 tbsp. flour
1. Take 1 1/2 cups milk
1. Get 3 cups old cheddar, grated
1. Get 1/2 cup FRONT STREET HEAT (plus more, to serve)
1. Get 4 cups elbow noodles, cooked el dante




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Front Street Mac and Cheese:

1. Preheat oven to 325°F
1. In a Dutch oven, over medium heat, melt butter.
1. Add onions and garlic. Cook until onions are translucent (2-3 minutes).
1. Add flour and stir until evenly mixed.
1. Add milk while continuously stirring. Continue stirring until sauce starts to thicken. Be careful not to burn.
1. Add cheese, stir and let melt.
1. Add FRONT STREET HEAT and combine.
1. Stir in cooked elbow noodles. Cover and cook in oven for 10-15 minutes, or until noodles are completely cooked.
